Hotels

The Chedi Adermatt

Located in a quiet area in Andermatt amidst the beautiful nature of the Swiss Alps, 850 m from the Gemsstockbahn Cable Car Station, The Chedi Andermatt combines Swiss hospitality tradition with Asian elegance. It boasts more than 200 fireplaces, an indoor and outdoor pool, a 24-hour reception, and free Wi-Fi. Free luggage storage is provided, and the lobby is equipped with 2 fireplaces. The Chedi Andermatt features a 2,400-square-metre wellness and spa area, comprising a fitness centre, a hot tub, a steam bath and a sauna. Massages are available on request. The pool area is fitted with padded day beds.

Granbaita Dolomites

Granbaita Dolomites offers views of the Sella Group and the luxury of a 5-star superior hotel. Its wellness centre includes indoor and outdoor swimming pools, and parking is free.Hotel Granbaita Dolomites features elegant and large suites with private balcony and satellite TV. Wi-Fi access is available in the lobby. It's restaurant serves local specialities, and has a list of selected wines.The Granbaita Dolomites is just in the center of Selva di Val gardena. The trekking and mountain bike paths of the Sassolungo Mountains are within easy reach.

D’Olde Kamp, Ansen, NetherlandsD’Olde Kamp, Ansen, Netherlands

Ansen, Drenthe is home to the beautiful Campsite D'Olde Kamp, an intimate campsite, located in a wood.In the hustle and bustle of everyday life, everyone needs space to enjoy the great outdoors and a little relaxation. And this campsite certainly offers you space. Camping offers which are marked out and pitches with some shade. Spaciously laid out camping pitches make it possible to camp in a quiet and pleasant way. A stay here will bring guaranteed rest, space and freedom.

Ski Resorts

Les Rives d'Argentière

Situated in the centre of Argentière, Les Rives d'Argentière offers 5* chalets each with a hot tub on the terrace, a heated ski equipment room, a heated pool, a sauna, a gym, a cinema room, a wine cellar and access to an underground car park.Additional facilities provided for your convenience include a washing machine, ironing facilities and a dishwasher. Upon request, guests can also benefit from room service, a chauffeur service and a concierge.

Grand hôtel des alpes

Situated in Chamonix, at the foot of Mont Blanc and at the heart of the Alpine mountains, the Grand Hotel des Alpes offers unique accommodation. Enjoy its stylish woodwork, comfortable lounge with a view of the Massif de Mont Blanc or relax at the bar next to the fireplace.This lovely location allows guests the opportunity to appreciate the typical charm of the city centre and the enchanting surrounding area all year round.The property is a ski pass vendor and also offers a free shuttle services to the Chamonix ski slopes upon request in winter.
